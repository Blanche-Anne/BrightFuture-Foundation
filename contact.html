Frequently Asked Questions

How can I verify BrightFuture's impact?

We publish annual impact reports with detailed financials and project outcomes. You can download our latest report from our website or request a copy by email.

What percentage of my donation goes to programs?

85% of all donations directly support our education and clean water programs. 10% covers administrative costs, and 5% goes to fundraising efforts.

Can I visit your project sites?

Yes! We organize donor visits to our project sites. Please contact us at least 4 weeks in advance to arrange a visit.

Do you accept in-kind donations?

We accept specific in-kind donations that align with our current project needs. Please contact us to discuss potential in-kind contributions.

How can my company partner with BrightFuture?

We offer various corporate partnership opportunities including employee volunteering, matching gifts, and project sponsorship. Contact our partnerships team for more information.

Are my donations tax-deductible?

Yes, BrightFuture Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) organization in the US and is recognized as a charity in all countries where we operate. Donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.
